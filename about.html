About GameVerse

A curated collection of unforgettable gaming experiences across every genre.

What is GameVerse?

GameVerse is a curated game collection website built to showcase some of the most defining titles across six core genres. Whether you're into pulse-pounding action, spine-chilling horror, or laid-back simulation — there's a world here for you.


Why these games?

Every game in this collection was hand-picked for its world-building, gameplay depth, or cultural impact. From indie gems like Limbo and Inside, to AAA behemoths like Call of Duty and Final Fantasy — this vault represents the full spectrum of what gaming can be.


Built With

Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks. No dependencies. Hosted on GitHub Pages with a custom purple theme, smooth animations, and full mobile support.
